About Us


WE ARE PASSIONATE ABOUT WHAT WE DO - SUPPORTING YOUR BUSINESS

Zycian Accounting and Tax Services was established in 2009 by founder Raeesah Velloo. Collectively our team has in excess of 20 years’ experience. From humble beginnings we have built a dependable and trustworthy accounting and tax service to the public. Although we have a relaxed approach, we are professional in every aspect, taking care in service delivery, courteousness, product knowledge and drive to support you in taking your business to the next level. With a zeal and sincerity for assisting businesses with their accounting processes and accurate record keeping, clients have been able to focus on growing their business. We believe in going the extra mile to gather momentum required for client’s expectation and outcomes. We are passionate about bookkeeping, accounting and your business. We love what we do and have an earnestness to provide the best quality service at the most reasonable prices. We speak in plain English and do not complicate matters with unpronounceable accounting words. We are knowledgeable and very good at what we do. Allow us to worry about keeping accurate records of your financial transactions, while you FOCUS ON GROWING YOUR BUSINESS

To deliver our highest standard of Accounting services and Tax advise resulting in excellent and accurate financial reporting.
